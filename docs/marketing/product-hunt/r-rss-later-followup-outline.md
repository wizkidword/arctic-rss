# r/rss later follow-up outline

Do **not** use this on launch day. A r/rss update was already posted on 2026-07-15. Revisit only after a substantive product improvement or meaningful learning period, and follow the community rules then in effect.

1. State the concrete update or learning, not Product Hunt rank or vote count.
2. Explain what changed in the reader with evidence/screenshots using public/demo data.
3. Ask one specific RSS-reader usability question.
4. Disclose maker affiliation clearly.
5. Invite criticism without asking for Product Hunt votes, promotion, or cross-site activity.
