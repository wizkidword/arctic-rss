## FAQ draft (truth-checked)

**Can I try it without an account?** Yes. Use Browse as Guest to explore public feeds and podcasts. Saving, following, subscribing, and AI tools require an account.

**Is it a native mobile app?** No. Arctic RSS is browser-based. The current public reader is designed to remain usable at mobile widths.

**What can I follow?** Public RSS sources, with Discover options for feeds and podcasts. Availability of a publisher’s full article body depends on that source’s feed.

**Is AI required?** No. AI summaries are optional account-only tools; the reader works without them.

**What is the price?** Confirm the current live pricing with the maker before submission. Do not infer it from repository code.
