# Human response guide

Respond personally; do not use automated reply templates or impersonated voice.

1. Thank the person for the specific thing they tried.
2. Confirm your understanding of their observation.
3. Ask one useful follow-up question when it would clarify the issue.
4. Acknowledge a bug or limitation directly.
5. Avoid defensiveness and avoid casual date promises.
6. Record feedback in the post-launch review with a reproducible context.
7. Close only after the person has what they need; do not steer toward votes.
