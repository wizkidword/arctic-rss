# Account and community checklist

## Human-only Product Hunt account setup

- [ ] Create or complete a **personal** Product Hunt account.
- [ ] Use the maker’s real first and last name.
- [ ] Use a clear personal photograph.
- [ ] Choose a personal username, not `ArcticRSS` or the domain.
- [ ] Write a genuine personal bio.
- [ ] Add relevant personal website and social links.
- [ ] Complete Product Hunt onboarding.
- [ ] Record `PRODUCT_HUNT_ACCOUNT_CREATED_DATE` in `human-inputs.md`.
- [ ] Calculate the first safely eligible launch date as **account creation date + 8 full days**.
- [ ] Confirm the live Product Hunt requirement before scheduling; the product’s current official policy is the authority.

Do not manufacture activity, use fake profiles, mass-follow, mass-vote, comment to hit a quota, mention Arctic RSS in unrelated threads, or coordinate supporters.

## Natural warm-up guidance

- Browse Product Hunt normally.
- Try products that are genuinely relevant.
- Leave a comment only when the maker has something personal and specific to contribute.
- Write each comment personally; do not use AI-generated text.
- Never treat interactions as a launch quota or an upvote strategy.

## Relative timeline

| When | Owner | Activity |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Now | Maker | Complete personal account and record creation date. |
| Account age days 1–7 | Product team | Repair/release P1s; prepare synthetic demo data and capture plan. |
| At least 8 full days after account creation | Maker + reviewer | Confirm eligibility, create Product Hunt draft manually, upload only reviewed assets. |
| 7–3 days before launch | Reviewer | Preview all submission fields, test direct link, review no-vote language. |
| 48–24 hours before launch | Release operator | Approved deployment, public smoke, asset capture, accessibility/mobile review, GO/NO-GO. |
| Launch day | Maker | Launch/schedule manually only when able to support it; reply personally and record feedback. |
| +24 hours | Maker + team | Triage feedback, status check, no ranking-driven changes. |
| +7 days | Team | Review qualitative learning and consented conversion signals. |

## Community permissions

- [ ] Channel owner confirms each community’s sharing rules.
- [ ] Any email list is verified opt-in under the applicable policy.
- [ ] External links use channel-specific UTMs only after consent/measurement approval; Product Hunt uses the direct canonical URL.
- [ ] The r/rss follow-up remains deferred until a substantive update.
