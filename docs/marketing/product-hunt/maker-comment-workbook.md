# Maker comment workbook

**Rewrite every sentence in your own words before posting.** This is a private preparation structure, not a ready-to-post Product Hunt comment. Write personally and only about what is true today.

1. Why I built Arctic RSS: `[personal reason]`
2. What frustrated me about algorithmic feeds: `[specific personal experience]`
3. Who Arctic RSS is currently for: `[honest audience]`
4. What works today: `[2–4 verified capabilities]`
5. What is still being improved: `[specific limitation / current work]`
6. Questions I genuinely want feedback on: `[one or two questions]`
7. Invitation: `[invite people to try the guest experience at arcticrss.com]`
8. Honest criticism: `[say directly that useful criticism is welcome]`

Facts the maker may verify in their own words: guest browsing exists; Discover has feeds and podcasts; accounts unlock following, saving, folders, and optional AI tools; Arctic RSS is browser-based. Do not mention native apps, chat, future promises, ranking, incentives, or votes.
