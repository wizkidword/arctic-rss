# Product Hunt policy sources

Checked for this preparation on 2026-07-14. Recheck immediately before manual submission because the live form and policies can change.

- [Preparing for Launch](https://www.producthunt.com/launch/preparing-for-launch) — direct URL, tagline, tag, thumbnail/gallery, video, scheduling, and feedback guidance.
- [How to post a product](https://help.producthunt.com/en/articles/479557-how-to-post-a-product) — manual post fields and account/onboarding context. It states a 260-character description limit.
- [Personal account vs company account](https://help.producthunt.com/en/articles/771527-personal-account-vs-company-account) — personal account is required for submitting, commenting, and voting; company accounts are not.
- [Can I ask my community to upvote?](https://help.producthunt.com/en/articles/484935-can-i-ask-my-community-friends-family-to-upvote-a-product) — do not ask or incentivize votes.
- [How do I share my post?](https://help.producthunt.com/en/articles/2690626-how-do-i-share-my-post) — avoid mass outreach, incentives, and coordinated voting; seek genuine feedback.
- [Commenting guidelines](https://help.producthunt.com/en/articles/10030102-commenting-guidelines) — personal, substantive participation; do not use AI-generated comments.
- [Featuring guidelines](https://help.producthunt.com/en/articles/9883485-product-hunt-featuring-guidelines) — products should be live, useful, and crafted; no vaporware.
- [How do I schedule a post?](https://help.producthunt.com/en/articles/2724119-how-do-i-schedule-a-post) — current scheduling window and draft behavior.

## Recorded discrepancy

The launch-preparation page describes a 500-character description while the how-to-post article describes 260. The copy package supplies both and defaults to 229 characters. The live submission form wins.
