# External announcement guardrails and drafts

Use only channels where the maker already has a legitimate, consented audience. Replace bracketed sections in your own voice. Never ask for an upvote, imply voting is expected, offer a reward for Product Hunt activity, or bulk-DM people.

## Personal X

> I’m sharing Arctic RSS, a calm browser-based reader for the sources you choose. You can try the guest experience at [direct Product Hunt page or https://arcticrss.com]. If you use it, I’d value honest feedback on [specific question].

## LinkedIn

> I built Arctic RSS for people who want a quieter way to follow publishers, blogs, communities, and podcasts. It has a guest experience so people can see it before creating an account. If this is useful to you, please try it and share candid feedback on [specific question]. [direct link]

## Personal newsletter / relevant maker community

Use a short, personal note about the product update and a specific feedback question. Confirm community rules first and share only where the maker already participates. Do not use a mass-message or vote-coordination tactic.
