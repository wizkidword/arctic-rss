## Qualitative review

1. What did people understand immediately?
2. Where did people hesitate in guest, signup, add-feed, individual-feed, or article-reading flows?
3. Which claim or gallery panel caused questions?
4. What mobile, content-rendering, or accessibility issue was reported?
5. Which three fixes are most valuable to qualified readers?
6. What should be changed in the permanent Product Hunt page, without making unsupported claims?
