# Manual YouTube upload checklist

Do this only after an approved final `demo-master.mp4`, timed captions, transcript, and thumbnail exist.

- [ ] Upload through the maker’s own YouTube account.
- [ ] Use the suggested title/description only after checking they remain true.
- [ ] Add `demo-captions.srt` and verify the final timing against the final video.
- [ ] Add the human-reviewed YouTube thumbnail.
- [ ] Do not leave the video private.
- [ ] Copy the complete, non-private YouTube URL; do not use a shortener.
- [ ] Confirm playback, captions, and thumbnail in a logged-out browser.
- [ ] Confirm the Product Hunt draft preview renders the intended video before scheduling.
- [ ] Never show credentials, private feeds, personal email, or production-only data.
